ABOUT US / OUR OWN HISTORY

Our Journey Through the Pages

In the heart of Småland, surrounded by dense forests and serene lakes, the idea of Boklusen was born in the winter of 2005. It all began with a simple passion: a love for stories. Inspired by the Swedish tradition of fika and storytelling, we set out to create a haven where books could connect people, no matter where they lived.

What started as a cozy corner bookshop in Älmhult quickly grew into something bigger. By 2010, we realized the potential of the digital world and took a leap of faith—turning Boklusen into Sweden’s first fully online bookstore. Our mission was clear: to make literature accessible to everyone, from the bustling streets of Stockholm to the quiet archipelagos of Gotland.

Today, Boklusen proudly offers a curated collection of over 100,000 e-books, audiobooks, and digital resources. But we’ve never forgotten our roots. Every recommendation, every title in our collection, is handpicked by our team of book enthusiasts, ensuring that each book finds its perfect reader.

Our Philosophy

At Boklusen, we believe that books are more than just words on a page—they are a bridge to new ideas, cultures, and perspectives. By combining the charm of traditional storytelling with the convenience of modern technology, we strive to create a platform where the magic of books can thrive in the digital age.

From the classics that shaped literature to the latest releases that challenge norms, Boklusen is here to ensure every story is just a click away.
